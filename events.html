1. Whispers of the Classics Book Club Gathering:

Date: Every third Thursday of the month

Time: 6:30 PM to 8:00 PM

Description: Join fellow bibliophiles in our cozy reading nook as we delve into timeless literary masterpieces. From Austen’s wit to Tolstoy’s epic sagas, we dissect the nuances, share favorite passages, and sip on chamomile tea. Whether you’re a seasoned scholar or a curious novice, this book club invites you to unravel the secrets hidden between the lines.




2.Ink & Imagination Creative Writing Workshop:

Date: First Saturday of each season

Time: 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM

Description: Calling all wordsmiths, dreamers, and storytellers! Our sunlit atrium transforms into a haven for creativity. Guided by seasoned authors, we explore character arcs, poetic forms, and the delicate dance of prose. Bring your musings, your tattered notebooks, and an open heart. Together, we’ll breathe life into narratives that echo through eternity.
